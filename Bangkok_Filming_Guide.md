# SnapThaiHub — Bangkok Filming Guide
**Send this to your Bangkok team member via LINE. One 7-Eleven trip = 2 weeks of TikTok content.**

---

## Before You Go

**Phone setup (takes 30 seconds):**
- Film in **vertical/portrait** (phone upright) — always
- Use the **rear camera**, not selfie camera — much better quality
- Turn on **Do Not Disturb** so no notifications pop up while filming
- Film in **1080p** if your phone allows (Settings → Camera)
- Make sure you have at least **2GB free storage**
- Best time to go: **morning (9–11am)** — good light, not crowded

**Where to go (pick 1–2):**
1. Any **7-Eleven** in Bangkok (Sukhumvit area looks great on camera)
2. **Tops Market** or **Big C** for snack aisles
3. A **Boots or Watsons** for the beauty section (Snail White)
4. A **night market** if convenient — extremely visual, great background

## The Shot List

Each clip = 5–10 seconds. No talking needed. Just film naturally.

| # | What to film | How |

## Bonus: 3 "Reaction" Clips (Most Viral Format)

Film yourself (or anyone) doing these:

**Clip A — "The Price Reaction"**
Hold up Mama Tom Yum noodles. Look at the price tag. Look at the camera. Look genuinely surprised. No words needed. (The joke is it's ฿7 — about $0.20.)

**Clip B — "The Haul"**
Put 8–10 snacks on a flat surface (counter, shelf, floor of your basket). Film from above, moving across each one slowly. Then tilt up to show the store behind it.

**Clip C — "The Find"**
Walk to the Snail White shelf. Pick it up. Show it to camera. Show the price. Mouth "what?!" or look shocked. No audio needed — TikTok will add music.

## Important Notes

- **Don't ask store staff permission** for casual browsing shots — just shop normally and film. Avoid filming staff directly or store security systems.
- **Do NOT film people's faces** without permission.
- **Shaky is fine.** Real, authentic footage outperforms polished on TikTok every time.
- **Film MORE than you think you need.** 30 clips → we'll edit down to the best 8.
- **Send everything via LINE or Google Drive** — don't compress before sending, keep original quality.

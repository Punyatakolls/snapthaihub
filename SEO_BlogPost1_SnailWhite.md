## Google Search Console Setup — Step by Step

Do this immediately after publishing your first blog post. It tells Google your site exists and to start indexing it.

### Step 1 — Verify your site
1. Go to **search.google.com/search-console**
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Click **"Add property"**
4. Enter `https://www.snapthaihub.com`
5. Google will give you a verification method — choose **"HTML tag"** (easiest)
6. Copy the `<meta name="google-site-verification" content="...">` tag they give you
7. Add it to the `<head>` section of your website's main layout file (your developer can do this in 2 minutes)
8. Click **"Verify"** in Search Console

### Step 2 — Submit your sitemap
1. Once verified, go to **Search Console → Sitemaps** (left sidebar)
2. Enter: `sitemap.xml`
3. Click **Submit**
4. Google will now crawl your site regularly

### Step 3 — Request indexing for new pages
Every time you publish a blog post:
1. Go to Search Console
2. Paste the URL of the new post into the search bar at the top
3. Click **"Request Indexing"**
4. Google will typically index it within 24–72 hours

### Step 4 — Check weekly
After 1–2 weeks, go to **Search Console → Performance** to see:
- What search queries are bringing people to your site
- Which pages are getting clicks
- What keywords you're ranking for (and not yet ranking for — which tells you what to write next)

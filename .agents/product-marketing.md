# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-06-17*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Anything from Thailand, shipped to your door — snap a photo, paste a link, or describe it, and we buy it and ship it to you worldwide.
**What it does:** Snap Thai Hub is a personal-shopper / proxy-buying service for Thai products. A customer submits any product found in Thailand (by uploading a photo or screenshot, pasting a shop link, or describing it), gets one all-in quote within 24 hours, pays securely, and the Bangkok team sources it — even from physical 7-Elevens, pharmacies, and markets — and ships it worldwide with tracking.
**Product category:** Thailand proxy-buying / personal-shopper / package-forwarding service. Customers search for "buy Thai products online," "ship from Thailand," "Thai snack delivery," "Thailand personal shopper," "how to buy [specific Thai product] abroad."
**Product type:** E-commerce service (special-order / proxy buying).
**Business model:** Quote-based. Price = product cost + service fee (from ~15%) + actual tracked shipping by weight/destination. Customer approves the quote before paying. Card payments via Stripe (live). Ships to 190+ countries.

## Target Audience
**Target customers (B2C):**
- **Homesick Thai expats / diaspora** living abroad who miss home products (strongest initial segment).
- **Travelers / Thailand lovers** who visited and want products they discovered there.
- **Thai food / beauty enthusiasts** worldwide (Thai snacks, K-beauty-adjacent Thai skincare).
- **Hard-to-find seekers** — people hunting a specific, discontinued, or physical-store-only Thai item.
**Decision-maker:** the individual consumer (single-buyer B2C).
**Primary use case:** Get a specific Thai product you can't buy (or can't find/afford) in your own country.
**Jobs to be done:**
- "Get me the Thai snacks/products I miss and can't find here."
- "Hunt down this specific / rare / discontinued Thai item for me."
- "Stock up on my Thai pantry & beauty essentials in one shipment."
**Use cases:** 7-Eleven snacks (Mama Tom Yum, Lays Thai flavors, Bento, Koh-Kae); Thai tea & coffee (ChaTraMue); beauty & pharmacy (Snail White, Srichand, Tiger Balm, Yadom inhalers); fashion/streetwear (Muay Thai gear); home & temple goods; literally anything sold in Thailand.

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** You want a specific Thai product but can't buy it where you live — local Asian groceries don't stock it, online shops won't ship it internationally, and many items (pharmacy goods, market finds, regional 7-Eleven snacks) are physical-store-only and not listed online at all.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Existing Thai forwarders are transactional and cold, and mostly only handle items already listed on Shopee/Lazada — they can't physically hunt a product in a store.
- Asian grocery stores abroad carry a tiny, generic selection.
- Resellers/marketplaces charge 2–4× retail.
- Asking friends/family to bring things back is unreliable and limited.
**What it costs them:** Overpaying for resold items, or going without; the nostalgia/craving goes unmet.
**Emotional tension:** Homesickness and nostalgia; frustration at dead ends ("nobody ships this"); doubt about whether a product abroad is even authentic.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** BuyYai, ThailandShopper, WeBuyBangkok, Thaifii, ThailandInTheBox — most are transactional and cold, limited to online-listed items, and none lead with the "snap a photo of anything" framing.
**Secondary:** Asian grocery stores abroad (limited selection); Shopee/Lazada international (won't ship many items, weak English support); having friends/family carry items back.
**Indirect:** Going without, or waiting until the next trip to Thailand.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- **"Snap it. We ship it."** — order *anything* by photo, screenshot, link, or description, including physical-store-only items.
- **Real hunting** — the team physically goes to markets, pharmacies, malls, and 7-Elevens.
- **One transparent all-in quote within 24h** — product + service fee + shipping, approve before paying, no surprises.
- **Warm, friendly brand** vs. cold transactional competitors.
- Worldwide tracked shipping (190+ countries).
**Why customers choose us:** Easiest way to get *any* Thai product (not just what's listed online), from a brand that feels human and Thai, with honest pricing.

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Is it authentic?" | We buy directly from Thai stores, pharmacies, and markets — the real product, not a reseller's. |
| "Are there hidden fees?" | One transparent all-in quote (product + service + shipping). You approve before paying; no further charges. |
| "What if you can't find it?" | If we can't source an item, it's removed from your quote — you only pay for what we can actually get. Nothing sourced = no charge. |
| "Can I trust a new brand?" | Secure card checkout (Stripe), full shipment tracking, responsive support at hello@snapthaihub.com. |

**Anti-persona:** Wholesale/dropship resellers chasing the lowest bulk price; anyone already in Thailand; requests for restricted/prohibited-to-import items.

## Switching Dynamics
**Push:** Can't find the product locally; overpaying resellers; local stores don't carry it.
**Pull:** Snap-a-photo ease; the hunt service; worldwide tracked shipping; one honest quote.
**Habit:** Asking friends/family to bring items back; waiting for the next trip; settling for local substitutes.
**Anxiety:** Trusting a new/small brand with payment; will the right item arrive, and will it be authentic?
